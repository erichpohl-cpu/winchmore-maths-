Full Scheme of Work

Year 9 Set 3 — Topics by Term

Click each term to expand. Objectives match the Higher GCSE specification.

🆘 Autumn Term⌄

Calculations, Checking, and Rounding

  • Add, subtract, multiply and divide decimals and whole numbers; multiply or divide by any number between 0 and 1
  • Use the product rule for counting
  • Round to the nearest 10, 100, 1000; to decimal places; to significant figures
  • Estimate one- or two-step calculations using rounding to 1 significant figure

Graphs: The Basics and Real-Life Graphs

  • Identify and plot points in all four quadrants
  • Draw and interpret conversion, fuel bill, and real-life straight-line graphs
  • Draw distance–time and velocity–time graphs; calculate speed, acceleration, and area under graphs
  • Find coordinates of the midpoint of a line segment

Polygons, Angles, and Parallel Lines

  • Classify quadrilaterals and triangles by geometric properties
  • Derive and use angle sums in triangles (180°) and quadrilaterals (360°)
  • Use the exterior angle theorem; use corresponding and alternate angles in parallel lines

Algebra: The Basics

  • Use algebraic notation; collect like terms; substitute into expressions
  • Use index notation and laws for positive, zero, and negative powers
  • Expand single and double brackets; factorise including quadratics

Elevation and Construction

  • Draw 3D shapes on isometric grids; draw front, side, and plan elevations
  • Use scale drawings and maps; perform standard ruler and compass constructions

Fractions

  • Find equivalent fractions; convert between mixed numbers and improper fractions
  • Add, subtract, multiply, and divide fractions and mixed numbers
🌻 Spring Term⌄

Collecting, Representing, and Interpreting Data

  • Understand primary/secondary data and sample vs population
  • Produce and interpret composite, comparative, and dual bar charts
  • Produce and interpret pie charts and frequency polygons for grouped data

Factors, Multiples, and Primes

  • Find prime factor decomposition using index notation
  • Find LCM and HCF by listing, Venn diagrams, and prime factors

Sequences

  • Find and use the nth term of arithmetic sequences
  • Use the nth term to decide if a given number is in the sequence

Setting Up, Rearranging, and Solving Equations

  • Solve linear equations with integer and fractional coefficients, including those with brackets
  • Solve equations with unknowns on both sides; change the subject of a formula

Linear Graphs and Coordinate Geometry

  • Plot y = mx + c; identify gradient and y-intercept
  • Find the equation of a straight line from a graph
  • Explore gradients of parallel and perpendicular lines

Percentages

  • Convert between fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • Calculate percentage increases and decreases; simple and compound interest; reverse percentages

Substitution, Rearranging Formulae, and Iteration

  • Substitute into kinematics formulae; change the subject of complex formulae
  • Use iteration to find approximate solutions to equations

Averages and Range

  • Calculate mean, median, mode, and range; construct stem and leaf diagrams
  • Estimate mean from grouped data using mid-interval values

Pythagoras and Trigonometry

  • Use Pythagoras' theorem in 2D; calculate hypotenuse and shorter sides (including surd answers)
  • Use sine, cosine, and tangent to find angles and lengths in 2D; find exact trig values
☀️ Summer Term⌄

Perimeter and Area

  • Use formulae for area of triangle, rectangle, trapezium, and parallelogram
  • Calculate area and perimeter of compound shapes; estimate by rounding

Circles

  • Use C = 2πr = πd and A = πr²; calculate arc lengths and sector areas; give answers in terms of π

Transformations

  • Describe rotations, reflections, and translations fully
  • Enlarge by positive, fractional, and negative scale factors; describe combined transformations

Probability

  • Write probabilities as fractions/decimals/percentages; use relative frequency
  • List outcomes systematically; draw sample space diagrams; use 1 – p for the complement

3D Shapes, Volume, and Surface Area

  • Find surface area of prisms; find volume of cuboids and prisms; convert metric volume measures

Standard Form

  • Convert numbers into and out of standard form; add, subtract, multiply, and divide in standard form

Ratio and Proportion

  • Write ratios in simplest form including three-part ratios; divide a quantity in a given ratio

Construction, Loci, and Bearings

  • Construct SSS/SAS/ASA triangles; bisect lines and angles
  • Construct loci defined by distances; solve loci problems with bearings

Cumulative Frequency and Box Plots

  • Construct and interpret cumulative frequency graphs; find median and interquartile range
  • Interpret and produce box plots; compare two distributions

Scatter Graphs

  • Draw and interpret scatter graphs; identify correlation; draw a line of best fit; interpolate and extrapolate

Histograms and Time-Series Graphs

  • Produce histograms with equal class intervals; construct and interpret time-series graphs
