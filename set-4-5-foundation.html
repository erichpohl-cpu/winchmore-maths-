OCR Foundation GCSE

Why We Use OCR for Foundation

🤝

Tailored for Our Students

After reviewing all major exam boards, Winchmore chose OCR because its assessment style and question structure better support the way our Foundation students learn and demonstrate their knowledge.

📈

Gradual Question Build

OCR questions build more gradually into extended responses, with clearer scaffolding that supports students who benefit from structured question progression.

🌟

Confidence-Building Format

OCR's emphasis on explanation and application allows students to show their understanding even when complex data analysis is challenging.
