📌 Set 3 Higher — the Higher GCSE specification delivered at a confident, structured pace across three terms.

Scheme of Work

Year 10 Set 3 Higher — Topics by Term

Click each term to expand the topics covered.

🆘 Autumn Term⌄

Polygons, Angles, and Parallel Lines

  • Calculate interior and exterior angles of regular and irregular polygons
  • Solve angle problems using algebra

Solving Quadratics and Simultaneous Equations

  • Factorise quadratics ax² + bx + c; use the difference of two squares
  • Solve quadratics by factorisation, completing the square, and the formula

Cumulative Frequency, Box Plots, and Histograms

  • Construct and interpret histograms with unequal class widths; use frequency density
  • Estimate mean and median from histograms

Graphs: Real-Life, Quadratic, and Cubic

  • Calculate line segment length; find the equation of a line through two points
  • Recognise and plot quadratic and cubic graphs; find approximate solutions

Linear Graphs and Coordinate Geometry

  • Identify gradient and y-intercept from y = mx + c and ax + by = c
  • Find equations of parallel and perpendicular lines
🌻 Spring Term⌄

Probability

  • Use experimental and theoretical probability; use Venn diagrams with union/intersection
  • Understand conditional probability; decide if two events are independent

Accuracy and Bounds

  • Calculate upper and lower bounds for all four operations
  • Apply bounds to perimeter, area, and volume; use inequality notation

Equations

  • Solve linear equations with integer and fractional coefficients
  • Derive and solve formulae from word problems

Fractions

  • Convert between recurring decimals and fractions; find reciprocals

Pythagoras and Trigonometry

  • Use Pythagoras' theorem in 2D including surd answers
  • Use sin, cos, and tan; know exact trig values; find angles of elevation and depression

Sequences

  • Find the nth term of quadratic sequences; work with geometric progressions
☀️ Summer Term⌄

Indices, Roots, and Reciprocals

  • Use fractional and negative indices; apply index laws and hierarchy of operations

Perimeter, Area, and Circles

  • Calculate areas of compound shapes; arc lengths and sector areas; answers in terms of π

Solving Quadratics and Simultaneous Equations (Further)

  • Solve simultaneous equations by elimination and substitution (including linear/linear)

Percentages

  • Solve reverse percentage problems; work with compound interest

Inequalities

  • Solve and represent linear inequalities; use correct notation

Ratio and Proportion

  • Write ratio as a linear function; use ratio for real-life contexts and currency conversion
