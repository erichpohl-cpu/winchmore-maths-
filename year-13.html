A Level Year 13

Full A Level completion — advanced calculus, proof, hypothesis testing, and further mechanics.

Modules

Year 13 Content

Click each module to expand the full topic list.

📐 Pure Mathematics⌄

Topics Covered

  • Proof (by contradiction, counter-example)
  • Partial fractions
  • Functions and modelling (composite, inverse, transformations)
  • Series and sequences (arithmetic, geometric, sigma notation)
  • Trigonometry (reciprocal, compound angle, double angle formulae, small angles)
  • Parametric equations
  • Differentiation (implicit, parametric, related rates)
  • Numerical methods (Newton-Raphson, iteration, trapezium rule)
  • Vectors in 3D (lines, planes, scalar product)
📊 Statistics⌄

Topics Covered

  • Regression and correlation (PMCC, hypothesis testing)
  • Conditional probability
  • Normal distribution (standardising, hypothesis testing)
  • Further hypothesis testing
⚙️ Mechanics⌄

Topics Covered

  • Moments and equilibrium
  • Friction and inclined planes
  • Projectiles (horizontal and vertical components)
  • Application of forces (connected particles on slopes)
  • Variable acceleration in 2D
