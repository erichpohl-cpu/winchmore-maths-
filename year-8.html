KS3 — Year 8

Year 8 Mathematics

Deeper algebra, ratio, geometry, and statistics — building on Year 7 foundations with growing independence.

Home › Year 8
📝 Year 8 maths marks an exciting shift in your learning. You'll revisit key ideas from Year 7 but go further — tackling more abstract concepts, applying knowledge to unfamiliar problems, and developing the independence expected at Key Stage 3.

Full Scheme of Work

Year 8 Topics by Term

Click each term to expand the full list of objectives. Mastering objectives are for Set 1 only.

🆘 Autumn Term⌄

Percentages and Fractions

  • Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions and vice versa
  • Recognise equivalences of simple fractions, decimals and percentages
  • Calculate a fraction or percentage of an amount; find equivalent fractions
  • Use all four operations with fractions and mixed numbers
  • Calculate percentage increase/decrease; find percentage change
  • Mastering (Set 1): Simple and compound interest; reverse percentages

Ratio, Proportion and Scaling

  • Write ratios in simplest form; divide a quantity in a ratio
  • Find distances from maps using scale; express scales as simplified ratios
  • Use unit cost and weight to find best value
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve direct and inverse proportion problems; complete calculations from a given ratio

Expansion and Factorisation

  • Simplify expressions by collecting like terms; expand a single bracket
  • Multiply terms with numbers and letters; factorise into a single bracket
  • Expand and simplify double brackets
  • Mastering (Set 1): Factorise quadratics (x² coefficient = 1); recognise difference of two squares

Forming and Solving Equations

  • Solve simple equations using inverse operations
  • Solve equations with unknowns on both sides and with brackets
  • Form and solve linear equations from contexts
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve equations with fractions; solve basic algebraic fractions

Inequalities

  • Know inequality symbols and solve linear inequalities
  • Represent inequalities on a number line
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve combined inequalities; understand effect of multiplying/dividing by a negative

Angles and Polygons

  • Identify types of triangles, quadrilaterals, and angles
  • Find missing angles in triangles and quadrilaterals; identify vertically opposite angles
  • Understand corresponding, alternate, and supplementary angles
  • Find interior and exterior angles of polygons (sum of interior angles formula; exterior angles sum to 360°)
  • Mastering (Set 1): Use algebra to solve missing angles of polygons

Speed and Real-Life Graphs

  • Draw and interpret conversion graphs and real-life graphs
  • Calculate speed, distance, or time
  • Mastering: Convert time using a calculator

Pie Charts and Scatter Graphs

  • Interpret and construct pie charts by calculating angles
  • Interpret correlation and draw a line of best fit on a scatter graph

Distributions

  • Find mean, median, mode, and range from raw data and frequency tables
  • Mastering (Set 1): Find modal class and estimated mean from grouped frequency tables
🌻 Spring Term⌄

Transformations

  • Reflect shapes in vertical, horizontal, and diagonal mirror lines; recognise lines of symmetry
  • Rotate shapes 90° and 180°; recognise rotational symmetry
  • Enlarge shapes with a positive integer scale factor; find centre and scale factor
  • Translate shapes using vectors
  • Mastering (Set 1): Enlarge with fractional scale factor; reflect in y = x and y = –x

Construction, Loci, and Bearings

  • Construct triangles using SSS, ASA, SAS
  • Construct perpendicular bisectors and angle bisectors; measure and draw bearings
  • Mastering (Set 1): Understand and construct loci; construct 60° and 30° angles

Error in Measurement

  • Use approximation through rounding to estimate answers
  • Find limits of accuracy of rounded numbers
  • Mastering (Set 1): Combine limits of two variables; understand upper and lower bounds

Standard Form

  • Understand and use powers of 10
  • Convert numbers into and out of standard form including negative powers
  • Mastering: Calculate using standard form

Decimal Calculations

  • Add, subtract, multiply, and divide decimals; solve money problems and harder word problems

Rational and Real Numbers

  • Recognise rational numbers, reciprocals, terminating and recurring decimals
  • Convert terminating decimals to fractions; convert fractions to recurring decimals
  • Mastering: Simplify surds and rationalise a denominator

Circles

  • Know the parts of a circle; find circumference and area using formulas (C = 2πr = πd, A = πr²)
  • Find diameter or radius from circumference or area
  • Mastering (Set 1): Find area/perimeter of compound shapes with circles, semicircles, and sectors

Pythagoras' Theorem

  • Understand properties of right-angled triangles; calculate hypotenuse and shorter sides
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve real-life problems and coordinate problems using Pythagoras' theorem

Volume, Surface Area, and Density

  • Find surface area of cuboids and triangular prisms; find volume of cuboids, prisms, and cylinders
  • Use the formula for density; convert between metric units of area and volume
☀️ Summer Term⌄

Straight-Line Graphs

  • Plot coordinates in all four quadrants; plot linear graphs
  • Recognise y = mx + c as a straight-line graph
  • Mastering (Set 1): Find gradient and y-intercept from an equation; identify parallel and perpendicular lines

Algebraic Graphs

  • Recognise and sketch quadratic functions; recognise cubic, exponential, and reciprocal graphs
  • Understand time-series and real-life graphs
  • Mastering (Set 1): Sketch transformations of quadratic functions; draw cubic and exponential graphs

Simultaneous Equations

  • Solve simultaneous equations by elimination; form and solve simultaneous equations
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve simultaneous equations graphically

Rearranging Formulae

  • Change the subject of a formula
  • Extension (Set 1): Change subject when the variable appears twice

Probability and Venn Diagrams

  • Use the probability scale 0 to 1; find probabilities from equally likely outcomes
  • List all possible outcomes systematically; understand relative frequency
  • Extension (Set 1): Understand mutually exclusive and exhaustive events; calculate probabilities from Venn diagrams

Trigonometry

  • Use Pythagoras' theorem; identify opposite, adjacent, and hypotenuse
  • Use sine, cosine, and tangent to find lengths and angles in right-angled triangles
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve problems using trigonometric ratios

Congruence and Similarity

  • Recognise congruent and similar shapes; show that triangles are congruent or similar
  • Find the scale factor between similar triangles

Sequences

  • Generate sequences and find nth term of linear sequences
  • Secure: Generate sequences from quadratic formulae; find nth term of quadratic sequences
  • Mastering: Recognise and generate geometric sequences

Algebra (Review)

  • Simplify expressions; solve equations including those with fractions
  • Mastering (Set 1): Solve basic algebraic fractions
