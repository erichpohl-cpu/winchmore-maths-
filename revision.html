Strategy

How to Revise Effectively

📥

Upload — Embed the Knowledge

Start by learning and memorising key formulas and concepts. Use formula cards, posters, and worked examples to embed the basics.

⚙️

Process — Practise Varied Problems

Work through a mix of questions, interactive exercises, and online quizzes. Immediate feedback makes sessions much more effective.

📤

Download — Apply Under Exam Conditions

Attempt past paper questions and timed practice. Self-assess honestly — this is where real learning happens.

📅

Create a Revision Timetable

Set up a regular, time-bound schedule focused on specific topics. Cover both your strengths and weak areas — no cramming!
