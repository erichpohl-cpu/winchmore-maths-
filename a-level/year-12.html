A Level Year 12

AS-level content — building the foundations of pure maths, statistics, and mechanics.

📚 A Level content follows the Edexcel specification. Regular past paper practice and independent reading are essential for success. Your teacher will provide lesson resources and additional links via Google Classroom.

Modules

Year 12 Content

Click each module to expand the full topic list.

📐 Pure Mathematics⌄

Topics Covered

  • Algebra and functions (quadratics, inequalities, surds)
  • Coordinate geometry in the (x, y) plane
  • Further algebra (binomial expansion)
  • Trigonometry (identities, equations)
  • Vectors in 2D
  • Differentiation (polynomials, optimisation)
  • Integration (indefinite and definite)
  • Exponentials and logarithms
📊 Statistics⌄

Topics Covered

  • Statistical sampling (types and bias)
  • Data presentation and interpretation
  • Probability (conditional, Venn diagrams)
  • Statistical distributions (binomial)
  • Statistical hypothesis testing (binomial)
⚙️ Mechanics⌄

Topics Covered

  • Quantities and units in mechanics
  • Kinematics in 1D (constant acceleration, suvat)
  • Forces and Newton's laws (connected particles)
  • Variable acceleration (calculus in context)
