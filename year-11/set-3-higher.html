Scheme of Work

Year 11 Set 3 Higher — Topics by Term

Click each term to expand the topics covered.

🆘 Autumn Term⌄

Pythagoras and Trigonometry

  • Use Pythagoras' theorem in 2D including surd answers
  • Use sin, cos, and tan to find angles and lengths; know exact trig values

Practice for Mock Exams

  • Dedicated exam practice using past papers and low-stakes testing

Constructions, Loci, and Bearings

  • Draw 3D shapes on isometric grids; interpret elevations and plans
  • Draw, measure, and calculate bearings; perform ruler and compass constructions

Percentages

  • All percentage skills including reverse percentages, compound interest, and change

Ratio and Proportion

  • Write and simplify ratios including three-part ratios; write ratio as a linear function
  • Use ratio for scale models, currencies, and recipes
🌻 Spring Term⌄

Polygons, Angles, and Parallel Lines

  • Derive and use angle sums in triangles and quadrilaterals; the exterior angle theorem
  • Find missing angles using corresponding and alternate angles

Probability

  • Use experimental and theoretical probability; draw sample space diagrams and trees
  • Use Venn diagrams; conditional probability; selection with/without replacement

Sequences

  • Find the nth term of arithmetic and quadratic sequences; distinguish from geometric

Cumulative Frequency, Box Plots, and Histograms

  • Construct and interpret cumulative frequency graphs and box plots; find IQR
  • Construct and interpret histograms with unequal class widths; use frequency density

Averages and Range

  • Mean, median, mode, and range from raw data and frequency tables
  • Estimate mean from grouped data; compare distributions using stem and leaf

Standard Form and Surds

  • Convert and calculate in standard form; simplify surds (e.g. √12 = 2√3)
☀️ Summer Term⌄

Transformations

  • Describe and perform rotations, reflections, translations, and enlargements
  • Describe combined transformations; understand congruence and similarity

Quadratic, Cubic, and Other Graphs

  • Recognise and plot quadratic, cubic, and reciprocal graphs
  • Find approximate solutions to quadratics from graphs

3D Forms: Volume and Surface Area

  • Find surface area and volume of cylinders, cones, pyramids, and spheres

Factors, Multiples, and Primes

  • Prime factor decomposition; find LCM and HCF by Venn diagram and prime factors

Setting Up, Rearranging, and Solving Equations

  • Solve all linear equations; change the subject of complex formulae
  • Use iteration; prove results in 'show that' questions

Solving Quadratics and Simultaneous Equations

  • Solve linear/linear simultaneous equations by elimination and substitution

Inequalities

  • Solve and represent linear inequalities; use correct notation
