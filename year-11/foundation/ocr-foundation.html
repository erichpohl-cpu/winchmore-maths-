Why OCR?

Overview

At Winchmore School, we want every student — including those on the Foundation pathway — to have the best possible chance of success. After reviewing the major exam boards, we have chosen OCR (J560) for our Year 11 Foundation students because its assessment style and question structure better support the way our students learn and demonstrate their knowledge.
📚

Gradual Question Build

OCR questions build more gradually into extended responses, with clearer scaffolding for students who need more structured question progression.

🌟

Explanation Over Data Analysis

OCR encourages students to explain, interpret, and apply knowledge. This is more accessible for Foundation learners than Edexcel's heavier data-analysis demands.

🤝

Confidence-Building

OCR is a more supportive route for students who need confidence-building and clearer question progression — helping every student perform at their best.
